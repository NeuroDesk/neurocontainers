Cartool is free
Cartool is free if you use it for public research and non-commercial purposes. In this case, you agree with the following
 Creative Commons BY-NC-ND license (full legal code here: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/4.0/legalcode).


Cartool is not free
Cartool is not free if you are either working with private funding (private research), and/or using Cartool for some commercial purpose (making money through its use).

In either case, you will need an explicit agreement with us, through UNITEC, the University of Geneva's Transfer of Technology office.
